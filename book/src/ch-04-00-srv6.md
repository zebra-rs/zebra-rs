# SRv6

## Cisco IOS-XR uSID Convention

The Cisco IOS-XR uSID convention partitions the 16-bit micro-SID space into
the following ranges:

| Range | Cisco Term | Purpose | Format |
|---|---|---|---|
| `0x0000 – 0xDFFF` | GIB | Global node IDs (uN) | high nibble 0-D |
| `0xE000 – 0xE063` | LIB (default) | IGP-dynamic local SIDs (uA) | high nibble E |
| `0xE064 – 0xFEFF` | ELIB | Operator-pinned static SIDs (uDT6 etc.) | high nibble E or F |
| `0xFF00 – 0xFFEF` | W-LIB | Wide-format local SIDs | high nibble F |
| `0xFFF0 – 0xFFF7` | EW-LIB | Explicit W-LIB | high nibble F |
